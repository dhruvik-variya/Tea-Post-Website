India’s largest Tea Café Chain

Chai is not just a beverage, it is the Nectar which gives wings to your imagination. The refreshing taste & aroma of Chai open up doors to the world of dreams. And Tea Post is the zenith of such a dream – to serve the best Chai in the world to all those who yearn for it.

We don’t want people to have just ‘a’ Chai; we want people have ‘THE’ Chai. At Tea Post, you get the Chai which sends a scintillating sensation down your spine. If you’re an ardent fan of Chai, this is the place to be. Just come and feel the Tea Post magic!

The cultural connect

Tea Post is the flag bearer in creating a Tea Café experience based on Indian heritage and cultural sensibilities. That’s why our style of making & serving Chai is quite distinct. Also, every Tea Post is designed with quintessentially Indian visual theme and artifacts. We also regularly organize activities and cultural events on Tea Posts across 40 cities to strengthen the cultural connect. Starting from music performance to stand-up comedy, dance performances, aerobics master-class, and so many other activities. We take immense pride in offering tea lovers a chance to experience and appreciate Indian art.
